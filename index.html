# About-Me

Hello, I'm Munthasir Rahman

I’m a self-taught full-stack website developer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh, with a passion for turning ideas into visually engaging and highly functional web experiences. I enjoy building responsive websites from scratch and refining them into smooth, modern interfaces that feel intuitive and purposeful.

Over the past year, I’ve been crafting web solutions using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Tailwind, GSAP, JavaScript and jQuery, while also leveraging PHP, MySQL and Laravel to power dynamic, data-driven functionality. I’ve worked with a range of clients to help bring their digital presence to life through clean design, thoughtful interactions, and reliable code.

I’m driven by curiosity and continuous learning, always exploring new technologies and techniques to push my skills further and create better experiences on the web.
